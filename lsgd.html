The Local Self Government Department (LSGD)

Self Government Department (LSGD) of Kerala is a government department responsible for the administration of local self-government institutions in the state of Kerala, India. The department was established in 1959 and is headquartered in Thiruvananthapuram.

The LSGD is headed by a Minister of State who is responsible for the overall administration of the department. The department is divided into several divisions, each of which is responsible for a specific area of local self-government.

These divisions include:

  • Panchayat Development
  • Municipal Administration
  • Urban Development
  • Rural Development
  • Disaster Management
  • Finance
  • Planning

The LSGD is responsible for a wide range of functions, including:

  1. Planning and implementing development schemes at the local level
  2. Providing basic amenities and services to the people
  3. Maintaining law and order
  4. Collecting taxes
  5. Disbursing grants
  6. Promoting social welfare
  7. Disaster management
